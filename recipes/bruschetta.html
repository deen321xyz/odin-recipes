Classic Italian Bruschetta

Description

Authentic Italian Bruschetta! Learn how to make a crowd-pleasing tomato bruschetta. The parmesan toasts take these over the top and you will love that drizzle of balsamic glaze

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French bread, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • â…“ cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 ounce Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Brush bread slices on both sides lightly with 1 tablespoon oil and place on large baking sheet. Toast bread until golden, 5 to 10 minutes, turning halfway through.
  2. Meanwhile, toss together tomatoes, basil, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl.
  3. Mix in balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper.
  4. Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted bread slices
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
